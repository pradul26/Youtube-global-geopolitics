CHANNEL IDENTITY: This channel explains major global geopolitical events and how they affect India's economy, security, diplomacy, and everyday life.

The goal is clarity, speed, and relevance, not academic analysis.

Audience: Indian viewers aged 18–40 who want to understand global events quickly.

TONE: Informative, concise, punchy, and "street-smart." 

Guidelines:
Avoid academic jargon
Prefer simple English
Speak as if explaining to a friend

Avoid:
ideological bias
sensationalism
excessive speculation

Preferred tone characteristics:

| Attribute  | Description              |
| ---------- | ------------------------ |
| Direct     | No long introductions    |
| Analytical | Explain cause → effect   |
| Contextual | Show global → India link |
| Fast       | Maintain quick pacing    |


STRUCTURE FOR LONG VIDEOS:

The Hook: Start with how this global news hits the viewer's wallet or security in India.

Examples:
fuel prices
defence security
trade routes
technology access
global alliances

Example:
"This decision in the South China Sea may affect India's trade routes and your fuel prices."

The Global Fact
Explain:
what happened
where
which countries involved
why it matters globally

(Source: https://www.orfonline.org/featured , https://www.lowyinstitute.org/ , https://www.internationalaffairs.org.au/australian-outlook/ ).
Focus on verifiable facts.

The Indian Response: 
What India is doing about it 
How this affect India
How it affects common Indian citizens
(Source: ORF).

The "So What?": 
A clear takeaway for the audience.
Answer:
Does this help India?
Does it create risk?
What should Indians watch next?
Example:
"If tensions escalate, India may benefit from supply chain shifts — but energy prices could rise."

STRUCTURE FOR SHORTS:
Maximum 140 words.
rapid-fire facts.

Structure:
Headline
Bold and slightly provocative.

Example:
"China Just Tested Something India Is Watching Closely"

Rapid-Fire Facts Example:
Fact 1 — what happened
Fact 2 — who is involved
Fact 3 — why it matters globally

Optional 1-Line India Angle

Example:

"India relies heavily on this shipping route."

Visual Style

Use:
simple geopolitical maps
headlines
key numbers

Avoid:
heavy text blocks
long charts

Upload Rhythm:

| Content     | Frequency            |
| ----------- | -------------------- |
| Shorts      | Daily / 3–5 per week |
| Long videos | 2–3 per week         |
